Photography

I click with devices lying in the extreme end of spectrum when it comes to 'controls'.

At one end is a 1970s built film camera 'Yashica electro GSN' which has manual control for - Aperture, Focus & Shutter speed. The other one is a smartphone (iphone SE), so has some automatic image processing features and less manual options

The device I pick depends on the place I am exploring. I tend to go with Rangefinder on relaxing journeys where I can take my time and set up the camera; And the smartphone when I have to worry about the weight of my backpack
